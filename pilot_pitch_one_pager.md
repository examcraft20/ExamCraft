## What is ExamCraft?

ExamCraft is a cloud-based exam operations platform built exclusively for educational institutions. It helps your faculty build question banks, auto-generate balanced exam papers, run structured approval workflows, and export print-ready PDFs — all from one secure, multi-user platform.

**No more shared Excel files. No more last-minute paper drafts. No more approval confusion.**

## Your Data is Safe — and Stays in India

ExamCraft is hosted on **Amazon Web Services, Mumbai Region (ap-south-1)**, ensuring all institution data physically resides within India.

- ✅ Compliant with **India's DPDP Act (Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)**
- ✅ Your data is **fully isolated** from other institutions — we use row-level database security
- ✅ No data is shared, sold, or used to train any third-party AI system
- ✅ You can **export or delete all your data** at any time, on request
- ✅ Pilot is on a **dedicated environment** — not a shared staging server

We are happy to sign an NDA or Data Processing Agreement before the pilot begins.
